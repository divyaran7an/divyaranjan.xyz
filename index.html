Mission

Starting from scratch is insanely tough, yet brave tech entrepreneurs do it time and time again. My mission is to find & help zero-to-one startups succeed on their journey to greatness.

I am a passionate product person with over 10+ years of proven experience in designing innovative product and user experiences that accelerate -the growth of companies. My catalyst into user experience was in 2011 when I decided to follow my passion for design and entrepreneurship and co-founded Darnster, an online social platform that enables people to share with others anonymously. The accelerated growth and learnings that come from running a startup are lessons and experience I still leverage to this day.

Since then, I've had the unique opportunity to be exposed to and work with a variety of global brands in the consumer industry, as well as the finance and blockchain.

My skill sets are diverse, which allows the flexibility to work across many levels of an organization, from leadership teams to tactical product teams to create meaningful solutions. I believe in remaining perpetually curious, and love big challenges and excel under ambiguous conditions. Of all the things I value most, it's working with great people while making really cool stuff.

I'm always open to connecting with interesting people from around the globe, so feel free to reach out and say hello.
